Quick Reference

Damage Formula

Each armor piece reduces damage by: (D - FlatDR) x (1 - RelProt). Damage passes through each piece in sequence.

Tier Interaction

When the attack tier exceeds armor tier, protection values are reduced. Plate armor is high-tier resistant and degrades less.

Penalties Stack

Movement speed, healing, hunger, and ranged penalties from each armor piece stack additively across all equipped slots.
