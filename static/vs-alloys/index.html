Quick Reference

Crucible Capacity

A crucible can hold up to 2560 units of metal, enough to produce up to 25 ingots at once, with 60 units left over.

How Alloys Work

Each metal must fall within a valid percentage range. The calculator keeps your mix within bounds automatically.

Nugget Math

Each nugget is 5 units. An ingot takes 100 units, so 1 ingot = 20 nuggets of the right mix.
