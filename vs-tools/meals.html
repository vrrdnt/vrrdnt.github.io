Quick Reference

Cooking Bonus

Cooked meals get a 1.5x satiety bonus. Always cook ingredients in a clay pot on a firepit for maximum nutrition.

Category Balance

Meals require specific ingredient categories. Meat Stew needs protein; Porridge needs grain. Mix categories for best results.

Servings

Each clay pot produces up to 6 servings. Satiety shown is per serving. More servings = more efficient use of ingredients.
