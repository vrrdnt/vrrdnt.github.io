Quick Reference

Nutrient Depletion

Each crop consumes N, P, and K from the soil. Nutrients start at the soil's fertility % and deplete based on crop type. Below 50% slows growth.

Fertilizers

Bonemeal adds P, Saltpeter adds N, Potash adds K and +15% capacity. Compost adds all three.

Rotation Strategy

Alternate heavy N consumers (grains) with light feeders (root vegetables). Use legumes (soybeans, peanuts) to restore nitrogen.
